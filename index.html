About Me

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in the Theory Group in School of Computer Science at Nanjing University, advised by Prof. Penghui Yao. My research lies at the intersection of quantum computing and theoretical computer science, specifically focusing on quantum information, complexity, and cryptography. I also have broader interests in classical pseudorandomness and derandomization.
